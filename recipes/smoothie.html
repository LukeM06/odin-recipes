Peanut Butter Banana Smoothie

A Smoothie Made From Peanut Butter and Bananas

A peanut butter banana smoothie is a smoothie great for breakfast! The flavor mixes seamlessly together and tastes amazing.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of peanut butter
  • 1 banana
  • 1/2 cup of almond milk

Steps

  1. Put the ingredients in a blender
  2. Mix in blender
  3. Enjoy!
