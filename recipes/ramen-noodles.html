Ramen Noodles

Microwave Ramen Noodles

Ramen Noodles are a life line for most college students and knowing how cook them is essential for surviving college.
If you know how to correctly heat up this dish, you will be one step ahead of your fellow classmates in college and be closer to getting your degree.

Ingredients

  • Ramen Noodles pack
  • Microwave
  • A fork
  • Some water

Steps

  1. Open the pack and pour the noodles into a bowl
  2. Remove the flavor pack from the noodles
  3. Add water to the noodles
  4. Microwave the noodles for the amount of time indicated on the package
  5. Remove the noodles from the microwave, add the flavor pack, and stir
  6. Enjoy!
